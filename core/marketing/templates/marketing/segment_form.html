Help & Best Practices
Segment Types:
  • Static: Fixed list of contacts that you manually add.
  • Dynamic: Automatically updated based on criteria (e.g., Lead Source, Region, Score).
Criteria Tips:

For dynamic segments, use the criteria field to define filters. Example: {"region": "EMEA", "lead_score__gt": 50}.

A/B Testing:

You can use segments as target audiences for A/B tests to see what content works best for specific demographics.
