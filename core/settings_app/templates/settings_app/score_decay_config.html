ℹ️ About Score Decay

What is score decay?

Score decay automatically reduces lead scores over time to ensure recent engagement is weighted more heavily than old activity.

Example:

  • Decay Rate: 5%
  • Period: 30 days
  • Lead with 100 points will have 95 points after 30 days
  • Decay continues until minimum score is reached
