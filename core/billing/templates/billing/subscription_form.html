Subscription Information

Subscriptions represent a user's recurring billing relationship with your service.

Key Fields:
  • Subscription Plan: The pricing plan they've selected
  • User: The user subscribed to the service
  • Status: Current state of the subscription
  • Status Reference: Dynamic status reference
  • End Date: When the subscription ends
  • Subscription Trial End Date: When the trial period ends
  • Stripe IDs: For payment processing
  • Active Status: Whether the subscription is currently active
