Lead Scoring Breakdown

The Lead Score (0-100) predicts conversion probability based on engagement patterns.

90 - 100 (HOT)

Ready for immediate outreach. Matches patterns of top 5% historical conversions.

70 - 89 (WARM)

High potential. Engage with personalized content or solution briefs.

50 - 69 (NURTURE)

Recommended for marketing drip campaigns. Low direct engagement priority.

Below 50 (LOW)

Keep in system for background nurturing. Minimize manual effort.

Win Probability (%)

Found on Opportunities, this percentage reflects the AI's confidence that the deal will close successfully. Key drivers include:

  • Historical Industry Win Rate
  • Deal Velocity (Stage speed)
  • Interaction Frequency
  • Decision Maker Engagement
