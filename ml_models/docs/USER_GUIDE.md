Welcome to the AI-powered sales cockpit. This guide helps you understand and leverage the insights provided by our Machine Learning module.

## 2. Meet your Action Agents
The system doesn't just predict; it helps you work faster through **Action Agents**.

- **Lead Nurture Agent**: 
  - If a lead hits a **Hot** score (90+), this agent immediately alerts you and your manager via Slack/Internal Notification.
  - For **Warm** leads, it automatically schedules a "Follow-up" task in your CRM calendar.

## 3. "Why did I get this score?" (Explainability)
Every AI insight comes with a "Why" section. Common reasons you might see:
- *Positive*: "High website engagement within the last 48 hours."
- *Negative*: "Industry historically has a 50% slower conversion rate."

## 4. Best Practices
- **Feedback Loop**: If you think a score is wrong, update the Lead data (e.g., adding a missing Industry or Company Size). The AI re-calculates the score instantly.
- **Trust but Verify**: Use the AI as a compass to guide your priority, but your human intuition handles the final relationship building.
